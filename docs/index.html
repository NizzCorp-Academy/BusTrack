About BusTrack

BusTrack was created by students at NizzCorp Academy as part of our capstone project. We noticed the challenges local travelers face when booking regional buses - long queues, uncertainty about schedules, and lack of real-time information.

Our app solves these problems by bringing the entire booking process to your smartphone. With features like live tracking, digital tickets, and multilingual support, we're making regional bus travel more convenient and accessible for everyone.

This project showcases our understanding of modern development practices, UI/UX principles, and real-world problem solving. It's not just a prototype - it's a fully functional demonstration of what students can build with the right guidance and creativity.
